Frequently Asked Questions

Creating an account is easy! Click on "Register" in the top navigation bar, fill out the registration form with your details, choose your role (Customer, Seller, or Admin), and click "Create Account". You'll receive a confirmation email once your account is created.

If you've forgotten your password, contact our support team through the Contact Us page. We'll help you reset your password securely. For security reasons, password resets require verification of your identity.

Browse our product catalog, click "Add to Cart" for items you want to purchase, review your cart, and proceed to checkout. Fill in your shipping and payment information, then confirm your order. You'll receive an order confirmation email.

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers. All payments are processed securely through our encrypted payment gateway.

Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days, while express shipping (1-2 business days) is available for an additional fee. Shipping times may vary based on your location and product availability.

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. Products must be in original condition with all packaging intact. Some items (like software and digital downloads) are non-returnable. Please visit our Returns page for detailed information.

Most products come with manufacturer warranties ranging from 1-3 years. We also offer extended warranty options for additional protection. Warranty terms vary by product - check individual product pages for specific warranty information.
