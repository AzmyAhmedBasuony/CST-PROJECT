Manufacturer Warranty

All products sold by TECHHORA come with the original manufacturer's warranty. Warranty terms vary by product and manufacturer, typically ranging from 1-3 years for most electronics.

What's Covered
  • Manufacturing defects
  • Hardware failures
  • Performance issues
  • Parts and labor
What's Not Covered

  • Water damage
  • Unauthorized modifications
  • Normal wear and tear
